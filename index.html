About Me

Hi! I'm Oluokun Favour, Oluwatobi, I'm passionate about health science and sustainable development. I have worked on multiple research projects to develop drug for treating various diseases as well as understanding the nature of pathogens. As an advocate for sustainable development, I run a company dedicated to pioneering sustainable access to healthy water while reducing plastic wastes, I have trained over 300 youths accross Nigeria on sustainable development, and how we can contribute to making our world a better place to live in.

Projects

Insilico Drug development for Trypanosomiasis

This research was carried out using Bioinformatic tools to identify a drug target and a potential drug for treating Trypanosomiasis. At the end of this research, we were able to identify silymarin as a potential drug for treating Trypanosomiasis

OWN-A-SCHOOL (United Nations-SDSN)

During the own-a-school project, I was able to train about 130 students on Sustainable development goals, and how they can contribute to making our world a better place. We were able to set up a club to further engage them in sustainable practices.

School Hydration Project

In order to drive sustainable access to healthy drinking water, we have installed TripeF hydration systems in various schools accross Lagos and Abuja to eliminate reliance on single-use packaged water and ultimately improve their health and environment.

5MILLION-SUPA

The aim of the the 5 5MILLION-SUPA project is to prevent 5 million single-use plastic wastes used in water packaging from polluting our environment annually by installing TripeF hydration systems accross Nigeria. So far, we have reached about 1 million single-use plastics and still counting.
